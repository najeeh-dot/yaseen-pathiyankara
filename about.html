About Velitcham

Velitcham Web Magazine is a dynamic publication that originated from the creative minds of students at Islahul Uloom Arabic College in Tanur.

Established as a platform for expression and information dissemination, Velitcham has successfully garnered a significant offline readership, boasting a substantial base of 1000 loyal subscribers.

Overall, Velitcham Web Magazine stands as a commendable initiative, driven by the passion and commitment of students at Islahul Uloom Arabic College, Tanur, showcasing the power of student-led initiatives in the realm of media and information dissemination.
